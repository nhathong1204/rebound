Meet Dr. Gary Blackburn, DC, FACFN

At Rebound SportsMed, Dr. Gary Blackburn brings over 30 years of expertise in sports medicine, clinical neuroscience, and brain-based rehabilitation to help patients optimize their health, function, and performance.

  • Experienced & Specialized: Over 25 years in sports medicine and clinical neuroscience.
  • Military-Trained: Began concussion management and performance enhancement as an Army Medic in 1986.
  • Highly Educated: 3,000+ hours of advanced training with certifications in Clinical Neurosciences and Functional Neurology.
  • Elite Consultant: Official Team Chiropractor for the Detroit Lions and consultant for the Detroit Tigers.
  • Olympic-Level Expertise: Completed a sports medicine rotation with the U.S. Olympic Committee at the 2012 London Games.
  • Award-Winning Three-time "Best of Kent" winner and three-time Seattle Met "Top Doc."
